About Agri Link

Built for Tanzania's farmers

Agri Link was founded with a simple belief: Tanzanian farmers deserve a reliable, transparent way to reach restaurants and shops — and vendors deserve fresh produce without the guesswork. We built a marketplace that makes that connection simple.

Powered by the mobile money infrastructure Tanzanians already trust.
